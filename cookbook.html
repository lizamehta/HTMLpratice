Recipe #1:Chocolate Bunnies

  • Time:About 30 minutes
  • Serves:5 People
Ingredients Quantity
Chocolate 1 bag full
Bunny mold 1

Step 1:Melt any type of chocolate of your choice

Step 2:Pour chocolate into the bunny mold

Step 3:Set the mold in the freezer for 30 mins

Step 4:Take out of the freezer and enjoy your delicious treat

Recipe #2:Frozen Chocolate Blueberries

  • Time:1 hour
  • Serves:two to three people
Ingredients Quantity Other Materials
Nutella 1 Jar Parchment Paper
Blueberries 1 Bowl Full A toothe Pic

Step 1:Wash all of the blueberries

Step 2:Put parchment on a plate and seperate the blueberries into rows

Step 3:Add a spoon or two of nutella in a heat safe bowl and heat it up in a microwave

Step 4:Using a tooth pic pick up the blueberries one by one and dip it in the chocolate

Step 5:Put the plate of blue berries into the freezer and let chill for half an hour or more
